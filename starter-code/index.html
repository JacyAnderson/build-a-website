About

Jacy Anderson is a web developer, musician, writer, and creative professional living and working in the Greater Denver Area.

Having graduated from Berklee College of Music with a Bachelor of Arts in Songwriting and Professional Music in 2016, Jacy has since moved back to his home state of Colorado after a year spent on the road with singer-songwriter Monica Rizzio. Jacy has made his home base in the Greater Denver Area, and begun developing a web development portfolio while studying at General Assembly Denver.
